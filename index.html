What is unblox?

The world of technology by large has been extremely insular. However, as technology becomes more and more pervasive in everyday lives, we believe that there is a growing awareness and need for an average consumer to understand different technologies and their impact on our everyday lives. The finance and economics of technology is something else that is usually not decoded for everyone.

With 3TB - three techies banter, our attempt is to decode this and explain it in as simple a manner for our audiences. We try and present, tech, the finance and economics behind tech in simple English. What gives us the edge in doing this is the fact that each one of us brings a lens which is unique. Our podcast is designed to help people converse around tech knowledgeably without jargon. Our fun facts and deep insights will help make party conversations more enjoyable with everyone being able to contribute to a tech conversation. We want to democratise the conversations around tech and make them a part of an average living room conversation – no longer will the world of tech be of tech, by tech for tech.
